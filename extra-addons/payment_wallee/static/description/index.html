The Wallee extension from PIT Solutions serves as a connector between Wallee and the Odoo Web Shop.

Wallee is a standardized platform for accepting payments through a wide range of payment providers worldwide. It supports payment gateways such as Credit/Debit Cards, PostFinance E-Finance, PostFinance Card, PostFinance Pay and TWINT. In addition to payment processing, Wallee offers a variety of features, including reconciliation. More information can be found under Wallee Payment Methods.

Our extension seamlessly connects Odoo shop users to a centralized payment hub that supports integration with over 50 payment gateways and processors. Powered by the official Wallee SDK, it ensures a secure, reliable, and well-maintained connection to multiple payment service provider APIs. This integration helps reduce cart abandonment by offering more payment options and lowers transaction costs through optimized payment routing.

The Wallee extension from PIT Solutions acts as a connector between Wallee and the Odoo Web Shop. It provides a standardized way to accept payments through a variety of payment providers worldwide. It also supports payment gateways such as Credit/Debit Cards, PostFinance E-Finance, PostFinance Card, PostFinance Pay, and TWINT. In addition to payment processing, Wallee offers a wide range of additional features, such as reconciliation.

The Wallee back-end provides a configuration where payment methods can be enabled for users. Additionally, the admin has the flexibility to specify whether the payment completion process should redirect users to the service provider's site or be handled within the shop site as an I-frame. Whether the gateway supports redirection, I-frame, or both depends on the payment service provider.

The admin can modify the notification content in the back-end for cases where the payment is pending, completed, canceled, or an error occurs. Other system notifications include a help message and a thank-you message upon order completion. Similarly, order emails, invoices, reminders, packing slips, and more can be edited to match the desired format.

Configurations

Once the configurations are complete, all the user needs to do is:

Add items to the cart and create an order.

Select the payment method.

Complete the payment transaction.

The order is placed upon successful payment.

Compatibility

The extension is compatible with Odoo version 18.0, supporting both Community and Enterprise editions.

Please note that for some payment methods, you are required to create a payment service provider contract with the payment service provider.

Wallee Provider payment methods support only the complete settlement of the bill amount and do not allow partial payments. Similarly, for refunds, only the full refund of the transaction amount is supported, and partial refunds are not permitted. If a partially paid bill is being completed, Wallee payment methods will not be available as a payment option on the payment page.
